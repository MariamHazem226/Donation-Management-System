Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Name, email, payment details (tokenized), phone (optional).

Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent.

Campaign Data: Organization details, bank info (verified), campaign descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process donations and payments
  • Verify campaigns and organizations
  • Send platform updates and receipts
  • Improve service and analyze usage
  • Prevent fraud and comply with laws

3. Information Sharing

We share with:

  • Service providers (email, hosting, analytics)

We never sell personal data.

Opt-Out

6. Data Security

We use TLS encryption and secure hosting to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed globally with proper safeguards.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of changes.
